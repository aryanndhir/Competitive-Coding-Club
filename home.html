Our Story

The Competitive Coding Club is a group of students that recruits and trains students to represent the MPSTME at the ACM Coding Contest. We strive to provide opportunities for students to supplement their learnings with out-of-classroom experiences through regular meetings, competitions, and workshops.

If you want to have a strong chance at getting placed at any of the top global companies then come join us. You don’t need to be a “pro coder” you just need to have the will to be one. So we welcome all levels of coders to join and code with us.

If you’re new to competitive coding then to become a member, just join the Discord Server and WhatsApp group. We'll update you about competitions and contests happening online and we'll compete in them together.
